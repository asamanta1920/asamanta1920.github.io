Education

Research

One of my research projects focuses on developing Protein Language Models (PLMs) for biological discovery and interpretation. In another project, I analyze dysregulated interactions across the various stages of Alzheimer's disease (AD) to identify specific genes and pathways for the development of AD therapeutics. I also utilize proteomic and deep learning methods to identify rapdily evolving genes in coral species in order to better understand coral bleaching. As an MIT PRIMES student, I have developed methods to aggregate distributed traces to make it easier for developers to gain insight into complex distributed systems.
